making a move

when it's your move, grab the mouse, and click one of the building troopers , and possible moves are shown in the blue circles. Once you click it a window apperas, if any player wants to vote hit Space or click the window

Voting

You decided to Vote, and you see a list of the Players Names, once one of you clicks on one of the Hands a raised arm is shown, next to that players name, indicating that this player gave a votum. Once each player voted the result is shown, and depending on that the trooper is moving over the board and/or it's the next players turn

Goal

Each Building of your type Yields points depending on it's size, the bigger the better
The Player with the most points wins.
So protect your own buildings by making clever moves that make it impossible for your oponents to reach them and use your right to vote to either distract or prevent damage to your favorite building type.
